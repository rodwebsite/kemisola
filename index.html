About Me

I am an experienced English Language lecturer with a Ph.D. from the University of Ibadan. My expertise spans Varieties of English, Pragmatics, and Semiotics, with a focus on creating inclusive classrooms for learners of diverse backgrounds.

Currently based in Clinton, MD, I continue to contribute to the field of education through instructional delivery and pedagogical research.
